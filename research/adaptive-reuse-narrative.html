Research brief

Adaptive reuse: narrative is the repositioning strategy

Obsolete assets can win when constraints become identity and modularity becomes the format.

Adaptive reuse case Modularity principle All research

Core idea

Reuse works when the building’s history is legible and the new use feels like a continuation, not a cover-up.

Three execution moves

  • Preserve a few high-signal elements (structure, materials, scale) to carry narrative.
  • Program the public realm like a venue: markets, events, rotating exhibits.
  • Modularize tenant formats to reduce repositioning risk and keep the destination fresh.

Underwriting note

Underwrite flexibility as risk reduction: lower downtime between tenant changes and more optionality for program revenue.
