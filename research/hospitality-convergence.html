Research brief

Hospitality convergence: service becomes rent support

When assets feel like venues, service isn’t overhead—it’s a revenue and retention engine.

Hybrid hospitality playbook Experience KPIs All research

What’s happening

Consumers increasingly evaluate places the way they evaluate venues: ease of arrival, service quality, and whether the environment feels curated.

Implications for developers

  • Underwrite the service layer (staffing + training + vendors) the way you underwrite capex.
  • Define experience standards across tenants: cleanliness, signage, music/noise, outdoor operations.
  • Plan for programming as an operating system: budget, partners, infrastructure.

What to measure

  • Queue time and service recovery time.
  • Dwell time by node (especially in public realm).
  • Revisit rate and referral intent.
