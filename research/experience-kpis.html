Experience KPIs: measuring the moments that make money

If you can’t measure it, you can’t improve it—and you can’t align design, ops, and leasing around it.

Download KPI list Tech-enabled principle All research

The KPI categories

  • Demand: footfall, dwell time, revisit intent.
  • Friction: queue time, wayfinding confusion, complaints.
  • Conversion: spend per visit, event-to-visit conversion, tenant sales productivity.
  • Loyalty: repeat visits, member retention, referrals.

How to operationalize

  • Pick 5–8 KPIs per asset and define owners (ops, marketing, leasing).
  • Create a monthly review rhythm and a “decision log.”
  • Connect KPIs to levers (events, staffing, signage, tenant adjacencies).
