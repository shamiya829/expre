SoHo House

A membership-driven model where programming and service standards produce belonging—and belonging produces retention and pricing power.

Hybrid hospitality playbook Hospitality principle Back to library

At a glance

  • Experience strategy Membership + community + consistent service
  • Tenant mix Rooms, dining, events, work, wellness
  • Design elements Residential feel, layered social zones
  • Financial logic Membership + utilization + events
Developer lessons

What to steal

  • Membership is an operating model, not a pricing tactic.
  • Programming is curated to reinforce identity.
  • Service standards are the invisible differentiator.
  • Spaces are designed to support weak ties and repeat rituals.
