Case study • Social games

Puttshack

A high-throughput social gaming format where booking, pacing, and service choreography protect experience while maximizing utilization.

Entertainment playbook Tech-enabled principle Back to library

At a glance

  • Experience strategy Games + social + F&B
  • Design elements Queue logic, bays, seating ladder
  • Operating model Booking, pacing, reset teams
  • Financial logic Throughput + spend-per-visit
Developer lessons

What to steal

  • Design the queue as part of the experience (reduce churn).
  • Use tech to reduce friction (scoring, pacing), not to add novelty tax.
  • Align service timing with gameplay to keep spend high.
  • Measure queue time, turn time, and spend-per-visit.
