Case study • Mixed-use

Avalon Alpharetta

Lifestyle district built around a walk loop, comfortable public realm, and programming rhythm that supports repeat visits.

Mixed-use playbook Placemaking principle Back to library

At a glance

  • Experience strategy District identity + weekly rhythm
  • Tenant mix Dining + retail + entertainment + residential
  • Design elements Walkability, plazas, comfort
  • Financial logic Stable demand supports leasing
Developer lessons

What to steal

  • Make the public realm comfortable enough to linger.
  • Use a calendar that creates habit (weekly + seasonal).
  • Design the loop so anchors are connected, not isolated.
  • Support day-to-night uses with lighting and safety standards.
