A flexible container for rotating experiences: modularity enables newness without constant redevelopment.

Modularity principle Entertainment playbook Back to library

At a glance

Developer lessons

What to steal

  • Modular bays make refresh cycles cheaper and faster.
  • Event infrastructure unlocks multiple revenue modes.
  • Make wayfinding part of the “world” without being confusing.
  • Build a partner pipeline like a programming team, not a landlord.
