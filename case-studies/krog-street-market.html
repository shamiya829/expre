Krog Street Market

Industrial authenticity + curated food: reuse becomes the brand, and the food hall becomes the community magnet.

Adaptive reuse brief Retail playbook Back to library

At a glance

  • Experience strategy Authenticity + curated F&B
  • Tenant mix Stalls, bars, specialty retail
  • Design elements Reuse cues, social seating, flow
  • Financial logic Utilization + repeat visits
Developer lessons

What to steal

  • Preserve high-signal elements to carry story.
  • Curate for trust: selection quality is the moat.
  • Design for comfort and acoustics (food halls fail here).
  • Use programming to keep the destination alive.
