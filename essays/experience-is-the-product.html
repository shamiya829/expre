Experience is the product

A definition that survives underwriting: the experience is what drives repeatable demand—and demand is what supports NOI.

Read the principles Open playbooks All essays

The definition

Experiential real estate is a system where design, operations, and tenant/brand strategy are deliberately aligned to deliver a repeatable experience people choose—then measured in behavior.

Three rules

  • Experience is not decor. If operations don’t support it, it’s a veneer.
  • Experience is not a one-off event. It’s a rhythm: weekly, seasonal, and ritual.
  • Experience must convert. Dwell time is only valuable when conversion points exist.

Developer takeaway

Underwrite the experience like a business: inputs (capex + opex) → mechanisms (flow + service + programming) → outputs (behavior + revenue).
