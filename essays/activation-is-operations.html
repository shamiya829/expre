Activation is operations

The biggest activation mistake is treating it like marketing. Activation is a system: infrastructure, staffing, partners, and a calendar.

Download calendar template Programming principle All essays

The operating checklist

  • Infrastructure: power, storage, access, ticketing entry points.
  • Budget: talent, vendors, production, marketing, security.
  • Partners: local creators, brands, community orgs, operators.
  • Standards: safety, cleanliness, service recovery, escalation.

Developer takeaway

When a team says “we’ll activate it,” ask for the org chart and the calendar. If neither exists, it’s a hope plan.
