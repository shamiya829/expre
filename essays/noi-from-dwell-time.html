How dwell time turns into NOI

Dwell time isn’t value by itself. It becomes value when the site has conversion points, a narrative mix, and operational standards.

Experience KPIs Download templates All essays

The chain

Dwell time → exposure → conversion → sales → rent support → NOI.

Three practical requirements

  • Conversion points: F&B, merch, ticketing, events, memberships.
  • Flow: circulation that repeatedly intersects conversion points.
  • Standards: comfort, safety, cleanliness, service recovery.

Developer takeaway

When you propose an “experience investment,” ask: which link in the chain does this strengthen—and how will we measure it?
