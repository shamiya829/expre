Experiential lexicon

Vocabulary the industry should use.

A shared language makes frameworks easier to adopt and improves SEO. Search terms below and link them from your principles and case studies.

Principles Tools

Glossary

Search by keyword (e.g., “dwell”, “flow”, “activation”).

Term

Anchor Experience

A primary draw that motivates a visit (not just an anchor tenant). Often a venue, iconic public realm feature, or recurring program.

Term

Dwell Time Strategy

Intentional design + operations to increase time-on-site, with conversion points that turn time into spend and loyalty.

Term

Social Activation Zone

A designed area for gathering, photo behavior, and events—supported by power, shade, seating variety, and clear sightlines.

Term

Circulation Flow Mapping

Overlay of paths, desire lines, dwell nodes, and dead zones used to adjust layout, signage, and tenant placement.

Term

Placemaking Layering

Combining comfort (shade/seating), identity cues (materials/story), and activity (events/vendors) to create a “stay” effect.

Term

Behavioral Design in CRE

Using behavioral signals (choice, friction, comfort) to shape layouts, operational standards, and tenant adjacency.

Term

Community Programming

A repeatable calendar of events and partnerships that makes the asset feel “alive” and builds revisit behavior.

Term

Hybrid Hospitality

Hospitality-style service layers (concierge, reservations, member events) embedded into non-hotel assets.

Term

Experience KPIs

Metrics that measure experience quality and connect it to performance: dwell time, revisit rate, NPS, queue time, conversion.

Term

Friction Tax

The hidden cost of confusing entries, bad signage, uncomfortable seating, long waits, and inconsistent service—paid in demand.

Term

Ritual Design

Designing moments people repeat: weekly meetup spots, seasonal returns, and “signature loops” that create loyalty.

Term

Tenant Mix Strategy

Curated set of uses that reinforce a narrative and spending pathway, balancing magnets, staples, and margins.
