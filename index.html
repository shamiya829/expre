What experiential real estate is

Experience becomes the primary value proposition—then design and operations reinforce it across every touchpoint.

Definition

Experience is the anchor

Not an amenity. Not an event series. The experience is how the asset competes and retains demand.

System

Design + ops + tenants

The spatial plan, operating model, and tenant mix work together to deliver repeatable moments.

Outcome

Measured in behavior

Dwell time, revisit intent, spend per visit, and social share become leading indicators for NOI.

Why it matters now

Choice is abundant. Attention is scarce. Real estate wins when it offers a reason to show up.

Demand is experience-led

Consumers optimize for identity, community, and delight—then decide where to live, work, and spend around it.

Behavior shifts Hybrid life Third places

Supply is function-heavy

Legacy assets are optimized for throughput, not meaning. Repositioning requires narrative, not just capex.
