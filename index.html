What experiential real estate is

Experience is the value prop. Design and operations reinforce it across every touchpoint.

Definition

Experience is the anchor

Not an amenity. The experience is how the asset competes and retains demand.

System

Design + ops + tenants

Plan, operating model, and tenant mix deliver repeatable moments.

Outcome

Measured in behavior

Dwell time, revisit intent, and spend per visit become leading indicators.

Why it matters now

Choice is abundant, attention is scarce. Real estate wins when it gives people a reason to show up.

Demand is experience-led

People pick identity, community, and delight—then decide where to live, work, and spend.
